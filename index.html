How It Works

1

Export from WhatsApp

Open WhatsApp, select the chat you want to save, go to chat settings, and export the chat. Choose whether to include media or not.

2

Import to ViewChat

Upload the exported chat file to ViewChat. Our parser will automatically extract all messages, images, and documents.

3

View and Manage

Rename your chat for easy identification, browse through messages, view images, and export any documents you need.
