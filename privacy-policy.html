Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

At ChatView, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We place a strong emphasis on your privacy. ChatView is designed to be a privacy-focused application that processes your data locally on your device.

1.1 Local Data Processing

All WhatsApp chat exports, messages, images, and documents are processed entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your chat data to our servers or any third-party services.

1.2 Device Information

ChatView may collect basic device information to improve compatibility and performance, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • Browser type (if using web version)
  • Screen resolution and language settings

2. How We Use Your Information

Since ChatView processes all data locally, your information is used solely to:

  • Display and render your WhatsApp chats in the application interface
  • Enable chat renaming and organization features
  • Facilitate image viewing and document extraction
  • Improve application performance and user experience

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • No chat data is uploaded to external servers
  • Temporary files created during processing are automatically deleted
  • Browser-based storage uses secure, encrypted mechanisms

4. Third-Party Services

ChatView does not use third-party analytics or tracking services. We do not share your personal information with any third parties except as required by law or to provide essential services that comply with this privacy policy.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your chat data within the application
  • Export: You can export your chats and attached media at any time
  • Delete: You can delete chats and cached data from your device
  • Control: You control which chats to import and rename

6. Cookies and Tracking

If you use the web version of ChatView, we may use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. These cookies are strictly necessary for the application to function and do not track your online behavior.

7. Children's Privacy

ChatView is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@chatview.com
